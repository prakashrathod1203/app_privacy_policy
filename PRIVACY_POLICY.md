# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 15, 2026**

Murli Dairy ("we", "our", or "us") operates the **murli-dairy-app** mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Application and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

### Types of Data Collected

#### A. Personal Data
While using our Application, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:
- Mobile Phone Number
- First Name and Last Name
- Email Address
- Delivery Address (Street name, City, Zip/Postal code)
- Password (stored securely and encrypted)
- Delivery Preferences (Daily milk quantity, milk type, delivery timing slots)

#### B. Location Data
We may use and store information about your location if you give us permission to do so ("Location Data"). We use this data to provide features of our Service, specifically to:
- **Auto-populate delivery address fields** during the registration and profile setup phases.
- Ensure accurate delivery routing for our dairy distributors.

You can enable or disable location services at any time through your mobile device settings.

#### C. Transaction and Delivery Data
We collect information related to your daily milk purchases and deliveries, including:
- Date and quantity of milk delivered.
- Payment details and method (e.g. Cash, Udhar, Bank Transfer).
- Balance history and payment status records.

---

## 2. Use of Data

Murli Dairy uses the collected data for various purposes:
- To provide, maintain, and support our daily milk delivery service.
- To notify you about changes or updates to your deliveries.
- To allow you to participate in interactive features of our Application when you choose to do so.
- To provide customer support.
- To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our Service.
- To monitor the usage of the Service.
- To detect, prevent, and address technical issues.

---

## 3. Storage and Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. 
- All authentication and API requests are transmitted securely using HTTPS protocol.
- User passwords are encrypted.
- We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, but we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

---

## 4. Disclosure of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your Personal Data to third parties. We may disclose your Personal Data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:
- Comply with a legal obligation.
- Protect and defend the rights or property of Murli Dairy.
- Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Service.
- Protect the personal safety of users of the Service or the public.
- Protect against legal liability.

---

## 5. Account Deletion and Data Retention

We will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.
- If you wish to delete your account and remove all associated personal data from our servers, you may request account deletion at any time by contacting our support team (details below).
- Upon receiving your request, we will delete your registration information, transaction logs, and location preferences within 30 days, unless required to retain it for legal, tax, or regulatory compliance.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
